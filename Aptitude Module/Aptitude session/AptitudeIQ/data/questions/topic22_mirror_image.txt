NOTE: For mirror image questions, the mirror is placed VERTICALLY on the RIGHT
      side of the figure unless stated otherwise. The image is reflected
      left-to-right (horizontally flipped).

SECTION A: MIRROR IMAGE OF LETTERS & NUMBERS (Q1–Q25)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q1. Which of the following is the mirror image of the letter "B"?

   Original:  B

   (A)  ᗺ    (B)  q    (C)  d    (D)  ꓭ

   Answer: (D) ꓭ
   Explanation: The mirror image of "B" flips it horizontally, reversing the bumps to the left side.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Q2. Which of the following is the mirror image of the letter "E"?

   Original:  E

   (A)  Ǝ    (B)  F    (C)  3    (D)  ∃

   Answer: (A) Ǝ
   Explanation: "E" mirrored gives a reversed E (Ǝ), with the three horizontal bars pointing left.

Q51. A clock shows 3:00. What time does it show in the mirror?

   (A)  9:00    (B)  3:00    (C)  6:00    (D)  12:00

   Answer: (A) 9:00
   Explanation: Mirror image of clock time = 12:00 − given time (for vertical mirror on right).
   Formula: Mirror time = 11:60 − given time = 11:60 − 3:00 = 8:60 = 9:00.

Q66. The figure shows an arrow pointing to the RIGHT (→).
   What is its mirror image?

   (A)  Arrow pointing LEFT (←)
   (B)  Arrow pointing RIGHT (→)
   (C)  Arrow pointing UP (↑)
   (D)  Arrow pointing DOWN (↓)

   Answer: (A) Arrow pointing LEFT (←)
   Explanation: Horizontal flip reverses left–right direction.

   What is the mirror image?

   (A)  Right angle at bottom-left
   (B)  Right angle at bottom-right
   (C)  Right angle at top-left
   (D)  Right angle at top-right

   Answer: (B) Right angle at bottom-right
   Explanation: Mirroring horizontally shifts the right angle from the left side to the right side.
