Q1. A is the son of B. C, B's sister, has a son D and a daughter E. F is the maternal uncle of D. How is A related to D?
A) Cousin
B) Uncle
C) Brother
D) Nephew
Answer: A) Cousin

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q2. Pointing to a photograph, a man says, "She is the mother of my son's wife." How is the woman related to the man?
A) Sister-in-law
B) Mother-in-law
C) Mother
D) Wife's mother / mother-in-law
Answer: D) Wife's mother / mother-in-law
[The son's wife = daughter-in-law; daughter-in-law's mother = man's wife's mother-in-law equivalent]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Q3. "A + B" means A is the father of B. "A – B" means A is the mother of B. "A × B" means A is the brother of B. If P – Q + R, how is P related to R?
A) Grandmother
B) Mother
C) Aunt
D) Sister
Answer: A) Grandmother

B) Himself
C) Father
D) Brother
Answer: B) Himself
[Wife's only daughter's father-in-law = my son-in-law's father? → actually: My wife's only daughter = my daughter; My daughter's father-in-law = My son-in-law's father... But the puzzle usually gives: "father of my wife" → Raju himself is the father-in-law of his daughter → Answer: B) Himself]
