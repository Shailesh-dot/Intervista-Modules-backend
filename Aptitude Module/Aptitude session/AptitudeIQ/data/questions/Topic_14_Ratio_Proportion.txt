Q1. The ratio of two numbers is 3:5. If each number is increased by 10, the ratio becomes 5:7. Find the numbers.
A) 15 and 25
B) 18 and 30
C) 12 and 20
D) 9 and 15
Answer: A) 15 and 25
Solution: Let numbers be 3x and 5x. (3x+10)/(5x+10) = 5/7 → 21x+70 = 25x+50 → 4x=20 → x=5. Numbers: 15 and 25.
