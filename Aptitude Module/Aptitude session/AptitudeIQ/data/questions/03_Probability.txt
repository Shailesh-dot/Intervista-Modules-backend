FORMULAE REFERENCE:
  P(A) = Favourable outcomes / Total outcomes
  P(AтИкB) = P(A) + P(B) тАУ P(AтИйB)
  P(AтИйB) = P(A) ├Ч P(B) [for independent events]
  P(A|B) = P(AтИйB) / P(B) [conditional probability]
  Complement: P(A') = 1 тАУ P(A)
