Q1. What is 35% of 240?
    (A) 74  (B) 84  (C) 94  (D) 64

    EXPLANATION: 35/100 × 240 = 84.

Q2. 48 is what percent of 160?

    ANSWER: B
    EXPLANATION: (48/160) × 100 = 30%.

Q3. A number is increased by 20% and then decreased by 20%. What is the net change?
    (A) 0%  (B) 4% increase  (C) 4% decrease  (D) 2% decrease

    EXPLANATION: Net = 1.2 × 0.8 = 0.96 → 4% decrease.

     EXPLANATION: Distance=speed×time=1.1×0.95=1.045 → 4.5% increase.

Q93. By what % is 40 more than 25?
     (A) 40%  (B) 50%  (C) 60%  (D) 37.5%
     ANSWER: C
     EXPLANATION: (40–25)/25×100=15/25×100=60%.

Q95. A's salary is 50% of B's salary, which is 40% of C's salary. A's salary is what % of C's?

Q97. The sum of two numbers is 100. One is 20% more than the other. Find the smaller number.

Q99. Find what % of a day is 3 hours 36 minutes.
     (A) 12.5%  (B) 15%  (C) 12%  (D) 10%
     ANSWER: B
     EXPLANATION: 3h 36m = 216 min. Day = 1440 min. 216/1440×100=15%.

Q100. A person's expenditure and savings are in ratio 4:1. If income increases by 25% and expenditure by 20%, find % change in savings.
      (A) 40%  (B) 45%  (C) 50%  (D) 35%
      ANSWER: B
      EXPLANATION: Let income=5000, expenditure=4000, savings=1000. New income=6250, new expenditure=4800, new savings=1450. % change=(1450-1000)/1000×100=45%.
