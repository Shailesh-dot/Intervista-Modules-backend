SECTION A: SHAPE TRANSFORMATION SERIES (Q1–30)
----------------------------------------------------------------

Q1. Series: ▲ ▲▲ ▲▲▲ ▲▲▲▲ ?
    (A) ▲▲▲▲  (B) ▲▲▲▲▲  (C) ▲▲▲  (D) ▲▲▲▲▲▲
    ANSWER: (B)

Q2. A square is divided into halves, then quarters, then eighths.
    The next figure shows the square divided into: ?
    (A) Tenths    (B) Twelfths
    (C) Sixteenths (D) Sixths
    ANSWER: (C)

Q3. Series: figure with 1 line of symmetry, 2 lines, 3 lines,
    4 lines, ?
    (A) 4 lines  (B) 5 lines  (C) 6 lines  (D) 8 lines
    ANSWER: (B)

Q4. Figures: a circle, a circle with a horizontal diameter drawn,
    a circle with two diameters, a circle with three diameters, ?
    (A) Circle with four diameters    (B) Filled circle
    (C) Circle with two diameters     (D) Circle with five diameters
    ANSWER: (A)

Q5. Pattern: a dot moves clockwise around a square's corners —
    top-left, top-right, bottom-right, bottom-left, top-left, ?
    (A) Top-left    (B) Top-right
    (C) Bottom-left (D) Bottom-right
    ANSWER: (B)

Q6. Series of shapes where each shape has one fewer side than
    the previous: octagon → heptagon → hexagon → pentagon → ?
    (A) Pentagon  (B) Square  (C) Triangle  (D) Circle
    ANSWER: (B)

Q7. Each figure shows a rectangle with an increasing number of
    horizontal lines inside: 1, 2, 3, 4, ?
    (A) 4  (B) 5  (C) 6  (D) 3
    ANSWER: (B)

Q8. A series of figures shows a spiral that gains one more
    revolution each time: 1-turn, 2-turn, 3-turn, ?
    (A) 3-turn  (B) 4-turn  (C) 5-turn  (D) 6-turn
    ANSWER: (B)

Q9. Figures: equilateral triangle rotated 0°, 60°, 120°, 180°, ?
    (A) 200°  (B) 220°  (C) 240°  (D) 260°
    ANSWER: (C)

Q10. A series shows nested triangles: 1 triangle, 1 inside 1,
     1 inside 2, 1 inside 3, ?
     (A) 1 inside 3  (B) 1 inside 4  (C) 1 inside 5  (D) 2 inside 3
     ANSWER: (B)

Q11. Figures: filled star, star with one point outlined, star
     with two points outlined, star with three points outlined, ?
     (A) Star with four points outlined
     (B) Star with five points outlined (all)
     (C) Fully outlined star
     (D) Star with three points outlined
     ANSWER: (A)

Q12. Each figure in the series has the shape of the previous
     figure PLUS a new triangle added to one side. Figures:
     triangle, diamond (2 triangles), trapezoid-like (3), ?
     (A) Pentagon-like (4 triangles)
     (B) Hexagon-like  (5 triangles)
     (C) Same shape
     (D) Triangle removed
     ANSWER: (A)

Q13. Series: circle, circle with inscribed triangle, circle with
     inscribed square, circle with inscribed pentagon, ?
     (A) Circle with inscribed hexagon
     (B) Circle with inscribed triangle again
     (C) Circle with inscribed circle
     (D) Circle with inscribed octagon
     ANSWER: (A)

Q14. Shading pattern in a 2×2 grid: TL shaded only; TL+TR shaded;
     TL+TR+BR shaded; all 4 shaded; then: ?
     (A) All 4 shaded   (B) 3 shaded (TR+BR+BL)
     (C) Empty          (D) TL shaded only
     ANSWER: (C) [pattern resets or reverses — if reversing: 3 shaded]
     ANSWER: (D) [if cycling: back to TL only]

Q15. A series: the shape gains one vertex each time —
     3 vertices (triangle), 4 (square), 5 (pentagon), ?
     (A) 5-vertex  (B) 6-vertex  (C) 7-vertex  (D) 8-vertex
     ANSWER: (B)

Q16. Figures show an arrow that rotates 45° counterclockwise
     each step: →, ↗, ↑, ↖, ←, ↙, ↓, ↘, ?
     (A) →  (B) ↗  (C) ↑  (D) ↖
     ANSWER: (A)

Q17. A dot inside a circle moves clockwise by 90° each step:
     top, right, bottom, left, ?
     (A) Left  (B) Right  (C) Top  (D) Bottom
     ANSWER: (C)

Q18. Number of lines in each figure: 2, 4, 8, 16, ?
     (A) 20  (B) 24  (C) 32  (D) 64
     ANSWER: (C)

Q19. Pattern: one large shape, two medium shapes, four small
     shapes, eight tiny shapes, ?
     (A) Sixteen micro shapes   (B) Eight small shapes
     (C) Four medium shapes     (D) Twelve micro shapes
     ANSWER: (A)

Q20. A series shows a clock with minute hand advancing 15 min
     each step: 12:00, 12:15, 12:30, 12:45, ?
     (A) 12:55  (B) 1:00  (C) 1:05  (D) 12:50
     ANSWER: (B)

Q21. Figures: an L-shape, a T-shape, an S-shape, a Z-shape, ?
     (These are Tetromino shapes) Next: ?
     (A) I-shape  (B) O-shape  (C) J-shape  (D) L-shape
     ANSWER: (A) [I-tetromino is commonly next in standard enumeration]

Q22. A series of hexagons where alternate segments are shaded:
     0 segments, 1 segment, 2 alternate segments, 3 alternate, ?
     (A) 4 segments    (B) 4 alternate segments
     (C) 5 segments    (D) 6 segments (all)
     ANSWER: (B)

Q23. Series: diamond (rotated square), square, diamond, square, ?
     (A) Diamond  (B) Square  (C) Pentagon  (D) Hexagon
     ANSWER: (A)

Q24. Figures: small black dot, small white dot, medium black dot,
     medium white dot, large black dot, ?
     (A) Large black dot   (B) Large white dot
     (C) Huge black dot    (D) Medium white dot
     ANSWER: (B)

Q25. A pentagon rotates 72° clockwise each step. After 5 steps
     it is back to original. Steps: 0°, 72°, 144°, 216°, 288°, ?
     (A) 360° = same as 0°    (B) 300°
     (C) 330°                  (D) 270°
     ANSWER: (A)

Q26. Figures show: 1 star, 2 stars, 4 stars, 8 stars, ?
     (A) 10 stars  (B) 12 stars  (C) 16 stars  (D) 14 stars
     ANSWER: (C)

Q27. Figures show stacked horizontal lines growing taller:
     Height 1, Height 2, Height 3, Height 4, ?
     (A) Height 4  (B) Height 5  (C) Height 6  (D) Height 3
     ANSWER: (B)

Q28. Pattern: rectangle → rectangle with rounded corners →
     oval → circle → ?
     (A) Square      (B) Oval
     (C) Pentagon    (D) Dot
     ANSWER: (D) [continues shrinking; or repeats]

Q29. A series of figures shows a triangle flipping: point up,
     point right, point down, point left, point up, ?
     (A) Point left   (B) Point down
     (C) Point right  (D) Point up
     ANSWER: (C)

Q30. Figures: 0 holes, 1 hole, 2 holes, 3 holes in a shape, ?
     (A) 3 holes  (B) 4 holes  (C) 5 holes  (D) 6 holes
     ANSWER: (B)
