TOPIC 15: PROFIT & LOSS + DISCOUNTS
TYPE: NON-PICTORIAL
STANDARD: Banking (IBPS/SBI/PO), SSC CGL, CAT, Campus Placements
TOTAL QUESTIONS: 100
================================================================================

KEY FORMULAS:
Profit = SP - CP | Loss = CP - SP
Profit% = (Profit/CP) x 100 | SP = CP x (100+P%)/100
Discount = MP - SP | Discount% = (Discount/MP) x 100
SP after discount = MP x (100-D%)/100

================================================================================
