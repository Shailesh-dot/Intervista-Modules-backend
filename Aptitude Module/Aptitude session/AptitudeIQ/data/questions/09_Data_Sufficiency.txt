       Type: NON-PICTORIAL (Text-Based Questions)

INSTRUCTIONS:
Each question has a main question and two statements (I and II).
Choose the correct option:
  (A) Statement I alone is sufficient
  (B) Statement II alone is sufficient
  (C) Both statements together are sufficient, but neither alone
  (D) Either statement alone is sufficient
  (E) Neither statement, nor both together, are sufficient

================================================================

Q1. What is the value of integer x?
    I.  x² = 49
    II. x > 0
    (A) A  (B) B  (C) C  (D) D  (E) E
    ANSWER: C
    EXPLANATION: Statement I gives x = ±7. Statement II alone doesn't fix x. Together, x = 7.

Q2. Is the number N divisible by 6?
    I.  N is divisible by 2
    II. N is divisible by 3
    (A) A  (B) B  (C) C  (D) D  (E) E
    ANSWER: C
    EXPLANATION: Divisibility by 6 requires divisibility by both 2 and 3. Neither alone is sufficient.

Q3. What is the area of rectangle R?
    I.  The perimeter of R is 36 cm
    II. The length of R is twice its breadth
    (A) A  (B) B  (C) C  (D) D  (E) E
    ANSWER: C
    EXPLANATION: From both: 2(l+b)=36 and l=2b → b=6, l=12 → Area=72.
