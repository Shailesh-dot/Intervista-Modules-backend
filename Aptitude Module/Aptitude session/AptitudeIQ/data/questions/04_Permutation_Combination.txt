FORMULAE REFERENCE:
  nPr = n! / (n-r)!         [Permutation]
  nCr = n! / [r!(n-r)!]     [Combination]
  nC0 = nCn = 1
  nCr = nC(n-r)
  Circular Permutation = (n-1)!
  With repetition: n^r (permutations)
  nCr + nC(r-1) = (n+1)Cr   [Pascal's rule]
