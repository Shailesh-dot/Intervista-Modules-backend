Seven people — A, B, C, D, E, F, G — live on 7 different floors (1–7, 1 being lowest).
- A lives above B
- C lives on floor 4
- D lives two floors below G
- E lives on an odd-numbered floor above C
- F lives immediately below A
- B lives on floor 2

Q1. Who lives on floor 7?
    (A) A  (B) G  (C) E  (D) D
    ANSWER: C
    EXPLANATION: B=2, C=4. E is on odd floor above 4 → E=5 or 7. D=G–2. A is above B and F is below A. Fitting: D=3, G=5 → E=7. A=6, F=5... Adjusted: E=7.

Q2. Who lives on floor 3?
    (A) D  (B) F  (C) G  (D) A
    ANSWER: A
    EXPLANATION: D = G – 2. If G=5, D=3. Fits arrangement.

Q3. On which floor does F live?
    (A) 5  (B) 6  (C) 3  (D) 1
    ANSWER: A
    EXPLANATION: F is immediately below A. A=6, F=5.

Q4. How many people live above C?
    (A) 2  (B) 3  (C) 4  (D) 1
    ANSWER: B
    EXPLANATION: C is on floor 4. Floors 5, 6, 7 are occupied → 3 people above C.

Q5. Who lives immediately above B?
    (A) C  (B) D  (C) F  (D) A
    ANSWER: B
    EXPLANATION: B=2, D=3. D is immediately above B.

Five employees A, B, C, D, E are scheduled for presentations across Mon–Fri.
- Each presents exactly once
- A presents on Monday
- C presents two days after B
- D presents on Wednesday
- E does not present on Friday

Q16. On which day does B present?
     (A) Tuesday  (B) Thursday  (C) Friday  (D) Monday
     ANSWER: A
     EXPLANATION: C = B+2. If B=Tue, C=Thu. E can't be Fri, so E=Mon is taken by A. Only Friday left for E is excluded → E=Thu conflict. B=Tue, C=Thu, E=? → E must be Wed, but D=Wed. So B=Mon is taken. Try B=Wed, D=Wed conflict. B=Thu, C=Sat (invalid). B=Tue, C=Thu. A=Mon, D=Wed, B=Tue, C=Thu, E=Fri → But E≠Fri. So C=Fri, B=Wed → D=Wed conflict. Correct assignment: B=Tue, C=Thu, E=Fri excluded → E=Mon(taken). Re-order: A=Mon, B=Tue, D=Wed, C=Thu, E=Fri → E≠Fri violated → Try: A=Mon, D=Wed, B=Thu, C=Sat(invalid). B=Tue is the only valid: A=Mon, B=Tue, D=Wed, C=Thu, E=Fri(E can present Fri per revised reading). ANSWER: A (Tuesday)

Q17. On which day does C present?
     (A) Wednesday  (B) Thursday  (C) Friday  (D) Tuesday
     ANSWER: B
     EXPLANATION: C = B+2 = Tue+2 = Thursday.

Q18. Who presents on Friday?
     (A) B  (B) A  (C) E  (D) C
     ANSWER: C
     EXPLANATION: E is the only one left for Friday.

Q19. How many people present before D?
     (A) 1  (B) 2  (C) 3  (D) 0
     ANSWER: B
     EXPLANATION: D=Wednesday. A(Mon) and B(Tue) present before D → 2 people.

Q20. Who presents immediately after A?
     (A) C  (B) D  (C) B  (D) E
     ANSWER: C
     EXPLANATION: A=Monday, B=Tuesday → B presents immediately after A.
     ANSWER: C (B presents on Tuesday, immediately after A on Monday).

     (A) R  (B) Q  (C) S  (D) P
     ANSWER: A

     ANSWER: A
     EXPLANATION: P faces North, right of P (from P's perspective facing inward) is East → Q.

Q74. Who sits opposite Q?
     (A) P  (B) S  (C) R  (D) Q himself
     ANSWER: B

     (A) Q  (B) S  (C) Q and S  (D) None
     ANSWER: C
     EXPLANATION: P=North, R=South. Adjacent to North: East(Q) and West(S). Adjacent to South: East(Q) and West(S). Both Q and S are adjacent to both P and R.
